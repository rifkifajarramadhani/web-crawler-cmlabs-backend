Figure 1: Illustration of the application of political campaign strategy examples in the digital era Political campaigns are a series of efforts made by party candidates in order to gather support and positive opinion from the public at large. null Therefore, the main objective of a political campaign is to get votes and win elections. During a political campaign, candidates or political parties will definitely try to introduce themselves and convey messages, values, and political visions and missions to prospective voters. Apart from the traditional way, candidates and political parties should consider more modern campaign methods, such as social media, advertising, and creating websites. The benefits that can be obtained include: 1. Wide Reach By implementing an online political strategy to win an election, a candidate can reach a wider audience geographically. Voters from different regions can be reached without geographic or time restrictions, allowing political candidates to reach a larger voter base. 2. Personalized Interaction In addition to the wide reach, one of the online political campaign strategy examples you can use is a personalized approach. Through an online approach, parties can also understand voter behavior and preferences based on accurate data, so they can optimize messages and target the right audience. This example of a political campaign in Indonesia also allows political figures or parties to interact directly with voters through discussion platforms or other communication channels. This allows for the exchange of ideas and builds closer ties between political candidates and potential voters. 3. Cost and Efficient Practices When compared to traditional campaign methods such as television advertisements or print media, online campaign strategies can be more financially efficient. Several online political campaign strategy examples offer more affordable options, such as paid advertising or implementing SEO services to increase visibility and traffic organically. By relying on digital marketing actions, online political campaigns will also be able to run in a measurable manner with the help of specialists. The following are some examples of political campaign strategies that can be implemented to support the previously mentioned benefits. 6 Effective Online Political Campaign Strategy Examples in Indonesia Political campaigns are activities that involve various strategies, including placing banners and billboards, delivering speeches and presentations, and participating in public debates. However, to support the achievement of a political campaign strategy that can meet public demand, the existence of a digital platform must also be applied as best as possible. Here are the steps to consider as an example of an effective political campaign strategy. 1. Brand Building in Digital Media Political figures will later act as leaders who represent the people. In order to gain people's trust, of course, good personality and political branding are needed. In addition to a positive image, political figures can gain added value if they are able to demonstrate readiness to respond to the needs and problems faced by prospective voters. Building branding can be done on various digital platforms. Branding that dominates social media and is in line with search engine marketing (SEM) has the potential to increase visibility and reach potential voters from all walks of life. 2. Know the Principles of Proper Positioning One of the main principles that needs to be known as an example of a political campaign strategy is being able to influence broad public perceptions. The principle of proper positioning requires politicians to be able to enter the top-of-mind category of a wide audience. In other words, this top-of-mind position indicates that an entity will be the first to be remembered by audiences in a certain context. 3. Prioritizing Research and Analysis According to the results of research by Line Today regarding the political campaign in 2019, it was revealed that as many as 80% of Indonesians aged 17–35 did not know the profiles of prospective political figures who joined the election. In your opinion, why could this happen? Broadly speaking, the lack of thorough analysis and research can be a fatal mistake in the political campaign process. The election of prospective leaders is a right for all people who meet the requirements. Therefore, market research and analysis are needed to find out the target audience of potential voters in order to achieve a unanimous vote. For example, if a political figure targets young people, then research and analysis must be carried out with an appropriate approach. 4. Actively Engaging in Social Media In this increasingly advanced digital era, the majority of people rely on social media as their main source for obtaining information. However, political information spread on social media is often not properly verified and is prone to the spread of fake or biased news. In fact, an average of 44% of users across nine social media platforms in the last year agreed that they felt safe doing activities on social media. But sadly, in 2023, the average data shows that user trust has fallen to 35%. The data shows that although it is considered the main source of information, social media can also be a cause of a loss of public trust in political activity. For this reason, parties involved in political campaigns are advised to produce credible and quality information, which can also be done through a special website. 5. Providing a Special Website Platform  Apart from social media, another example of an effective political campaign strategy in the digital era is to provide a special website platform. Websites can be used to clarify the extent of transparency and accountability of political figures to potential voters. In addition, with the existence of a campaign-specific website, you can also produce relevant and interesting content for generations who actively access digital media. You can share breaking news, infographics, or simply positive political activities with potential voters. However, in order to maximize its reach, the website should come with a user interface (UI) that is easy for users to navigate. The easy navigation process can later improve the user experience (UX) so that potential voters can spend a long time finding out about your profile. In order to improve performance, websites for political activities also require regular supervision by a team of SEO specialists and developers. 6. Presenting Informative Political Campaign Articles One example of an effective political campaign strategy is one that can continuously update the basis of communication with prospective voters. This goal can be achieved through the production of informative articles. By producing article content during the campaign period, political figures can increase exposure and branding simultaneously. The presentation of informative articles can be shared through a special website for political branding, as previously discussed. Apart from presenting actual and factual information, the articles presented must also comply with SEO principles so that they can appear on the SERP.
