Industrial on Small and Medium Enterprise Competition 2018 September 27th, 2018 11/11/2018 – 14/11/2018 All Day Jl. MT Haryono 167, Malang, Teknik Industri Universitas Brawijaya Lomba Industrial on Small and Medium Enterprise Competition (iSMEC) 2018 Industrial Engineering Universitas Brawijaya is proud to present an industrial engineering scientific competition with the theme “Developing a Sustainable Competitive Advantage of Edu-Tourism Business Process in the Industry 4.0 Era“. ISMEC 2018 participants are teams with 3 undergraduate students, where at least 1 member is an Industrial Engineering student or equivalent (Engineering Management, Industrial Management, etc.). The 2018 iSMEC activity is divided into online registration, online selection, and the final round where participants will take part in industrial visit, industrial knowledge and running laboratory activities. The implementation date of the iSMEC 2018 competition is as follows: 1. Online registration: August, 17th – September, 21st 2018 2. Online selection: September, 24th – 26th 2018 3. Finalist announcement: October, 6th 2018 4. Final round: November, 11th – 14th 2018 DARE TO BE THE WINNER? Prepare your best team and win a total prize of IDR 30,000,000.
