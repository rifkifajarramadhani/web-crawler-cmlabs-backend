Soekarno No.120, Batu, Singhasari Resort and Convention Seminar Internasional Portsmouth-Brawijaya Centre for Global Health, Population, and Policy presents: South-East Asia Biennial Conference on Population and Health Call for Abstract Papers and Poster Presentation Call for Paper Submit short abstract in no more than 200 words that include the description of the study, the summary of the main part of the presentation, five keywords, and references. Authors also asked to identify who will present the paper. Abstract should be written in English and submitted before July 1st, 2018 with subject line “Submitted for Papers” to seaaconference2018@gmail.com Provide full name, affiliation, country and email addresses of each coauthor for each submission. Full paper must be submitted after receiving notification of abstract acceptance, written in english, by October 10th, 2018. Selected Papers will be published in Asian Population Journal, International Journal of Population and Reproductive Health, Journal of Population Research (requested), and Journal of Migration and Development Call for Poster Submit abstracts/papers for consideration as posters by July 1st, 2018. All of the above instructions for abstract/paper submissions also apply for poster submissions. Author who wish to submit their abstract for consideration only for a poster session should submit ther abstracts/papers to poster session with Subject Line “Submitted for Poster“. Authors whose submitting paper to regular session may also have their paper considered for a poster session if the paper deemed to be not accepted for regular session.
