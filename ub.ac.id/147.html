International Conference of Inequalities, Social Justice, and Democracy Oktober 24th, 2018 26/11/2018 All Day Universitas Brawijaya International Conference of Inequalities, Social Justice, and Democracy The upcoming 1st ISJD looks forward to invite up-to-date ideas and research about the injustice and the inequality of social, politic, culture and economic problems as an effort to realize welfare and social justice in a democratic climate. Call for papers Prospective authors are kindly invited to submit their full paper. Articles submitted should meet the theme of “Social Justice and Democratic” and address to one more specific topics. The articles submitted also should meet the author guideline and the paper’s template of ISJD conference which can be seen in http://isjd.fisip.ub.ac.id. All accepted papers will be published on ISBN Book.
