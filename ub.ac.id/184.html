International Conference on Sustainable Aquatic Resources (ICoSAR) Mei 23rd, 2019 ICoSAR is an annual international conference organized by Coastal and Marine Research Center and Halal Thoyib Science Center, Brawijaya University. ICoSAR stands for International Conference on Sustainable Aquatic Resources. It provides a platform to exchange knowledge regarding development of aquatic food resources and marine ecosystems through sustainable fisheries and aquaculture. Theme: “Increasing OHI (Ocean Health Index) Through Sustainable Aquatic-Food Resources Utilization” with a focus on halal and Seafood. Sub-theme: Halal Management and Food Science, Seafood Safety, Processing and Engineering, Aquatic Biodiversity, Biology and Conservation, Aquaculture Sustainability, Aquatic Biotechnology and Pharmacy, and Fisheries of Social Economy, Law and Development Policy, and Oceanic and Aquatic Engineering. ICoSAR 2019 will be held on: August 27th – 28th, 2019 in Sahid Montana II, Malang, East Java, Indonesia.   Keynote Speakers Prof. Soottawat Benjakul, Ph.D. – Prince of Songkla University, Thailand Prof. Sukoso, Ph.D. – Halal Product Assurance Organizing Agency (BPJPH), Ministry of Religious Affair, Indonesia Prof. Ts.
