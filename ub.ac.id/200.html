How to Write and present research in English Workshop by UIG English@UB (English) Oktober 18th, 2019 Dear Research Colleagues, Are you applying for a PHD or wanting to present at an international conference? Good news! Our partner UTS Insearch – Australia has a workshop that will help you develop your capabilities in English for research purposes. Enroll in our workshop ‘How to Write and present research in English’ on 2 – 13 December 2019. Who is this workshop for? Anyone who wants to: • apply for a PhD • attend and present at conferences • write conference papers, research proposals, journal articles and progress reports for research projects • communicate more effectively and professionally with their peers and build a strong network of international academics • Find employment opportunities in universities where English is the primary language of instruction and communication. About UTS Insearch UTS Insearch is the premier pathway provider of the University of Technology Sydney, Australia’s No. 1 young University, and has worked with practicing research academics to bring you a workshop aimed at developing English for Research purposes. Workshop Structure Targeted at academics and prospective PhD students who are looking to expand their research influence and identify potential collaborators and/or supervisors. This two-week workshop covers: • Introduction to Research Writing: Texts, Contexts and Purposes • Research Ethics and Integrity • Reading and Writing Literature Reviews • Data Skills and Management • The Drafting Process • Presenting a Conference Paper in English • Writing and Submitting a Journal Article in English This Workshop includes directed teaching as well as self-study. In addition, participants will have the opportunity to work with the teacher and others, on drafts of their own research writing, conference presentations or any other type of research writing or communication that they are working on. English Requirement For this workshop an IELTS 6.0 overall with a minimum of 6 in Writing is needed. If a participant does not have an IELTS score, UIG will arrange a placement test. Workshop Details and Fee Workshop Fee: Rp. 4.500.000/participant Early Bird Registration of Rp. 4.000.000 open until 31 October 2019 Number of Participants/workshop: Minimum of 10 per participants and maximum of 20.
