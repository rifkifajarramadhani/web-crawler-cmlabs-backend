Seminar Internasional The 4th ICWRDEP 2021 April 9th, 2021 Universitas Brawijaya will hold the 4th International Conference of Water Resources Development and Environmental Protection (4th ICWRDEP 2021) on August 7, 2021. This conference provides a venue for participants to expand their global perspective and insight into water resources’ newest research. The previous ICWRDEP was successfully held with more than 200 participants from Indonesia, Malaysia, Thailand, Taiwan, to Japan by various universities and affiliations. This year, the conference will be held virtually due to safety reasons. Participants are welcome to share their experience on water resources development and environmental protection. Prospective authors are invited to submit abstracts through the website. There will be speeches from excellent and professional speakers across the world.
