Invitation to Bids on Project: University Teaching Hospital of the UB Malang Mei 12th, 2023 INVITATION FOR BIDS Country: Republic of Indonesia Project: University Teaching Hospital of the UB Malang Deadline: Jun, 12th 2023 Reference Number: BMZ 301000959 / KfW 507081 The government of Indonesia has requested KfW to provide a promotional loan amounting up to 37.0 million EUR for the expansion and upgrading of the Universitas Brawijaya Hospital (“RSUB”) with its Faculties of Medicine and Dentistry in Malang, to become an enhanced teaching facility for the education and practical training of medical students and other health professions. The Ministry of Education, Culture, Research and Technology invites sealed Bids from eligible Bidders for Consulting Services for the project the strengthening of the quality of health care services, and education for the healthcare professional education, especially medical doctors and dentist of the Universitas Brawijaya by establishing a modern 150 beds university teaching hospital that is Rumah Sakit Universitas Brawijaya with the medium-term perspective to become a class B hospital; and supporting the provision of medical equipment for the development of Universitas Brawijaya Dental Hospital (RSGM-UB). The prequalification procedure of experienced Consultants will follow the KfW “Guidelines for Consulting Services, Works, Plant, Goods and Non-Consulting Services in Financial Cooperation with Partner Countries which are available on KfW’s internet web-site (version January 2021): https://www.kfw-entwicklungsbank.de/PDF/Download-Center/PDF-Dokumente-Richtlinien/FZ-Vergaberichtlinien-V-2021-EN.pdf It is foreseen to award the services to an internationally operating consulting firm, which is highly experienced in the concerned fields with an annual turnover of the lead consultant and all associated partners showing the necessary annual turnover for the consortium as a group of at least EUR 1.2 million. An association with a domestic consultant is highly appreciated. The expected project duration is 36 months. Further Information : The pre-qualification documents (only available in English language) is available free of charge from the Coordinator of the Tender Committee and with copy to the Tender Agent, upon written request at the email addresses ( piu_phln@ub.ac.id ). The documents will be sent electronically only (by e-mail). No liability will be accepted for loss or late delivery. A virtual pre-submission information meeting to advise interested bidders on the formal submission requirements under this tender will be held on May, 19th, 2023 at 9.00 am Central European Summer time (CEST) or 2.00 pm Western Indonesian time (WIB).
