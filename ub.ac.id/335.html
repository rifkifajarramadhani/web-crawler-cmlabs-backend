The 1st Aquatic Science International Conference 2022 (ASIC 2022) September 5th, 2022 October 7th – 8th, 2022 “DROP OF LIFE: Challenge to Conserve Aquatic Ecosystems for Next Generation” We are pleased to invite you to the THE 1st AQUATIC SCIENCE INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE 2022 (ASIC 2022). It is scheduled on Friday, October 7th, 2022 – Saturday,  October 8 th, 2022, virtually using Zoom meeting. De Leon (University of the Philippines Los Baños, PHILIPPINES) Prof. Ir.
