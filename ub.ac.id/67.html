Final Call for Papers EECCIS 2022 Mei 24th, 2022 “Enabling Technology for Society 5.0” The 11th Electrical Power, Electronics, Communications, Control, and Informatics Seminar (EECCIS) that will be held in Malang, East Java, Indonesia. EECCIS 2022 will be conducted as a Hybrid conference from August 23rd to 25th 2022. This event is the biannual international conference, jointly organized by Universitas Brawijaya and Universiti Tun Hussein Onn Malaysia (UTHM). EECCIS aims to strengthen the collaboration and also provide a forum for academicians, professionals, and researchers to exchange their research results, innovative ideas, and solutions involving, including experiences in all aspects of intelligent and advanced technologies. Accepted papers will be submitted for inclusion into IEEE Xplore subject to meeting IEEE Xplore’s scope and quality requirements.
