The 13th International Conference on Global Resource Conservation (ICGRC) Juni 3rd, 2022 We are pleased to invite you to the 13th International Conference on Global Resource Conservation (13th ICGRC 2022) virtual conference. It is scheduled on Monday, July 25th 2022 – Tuesday, July 26th 2022 virtually using Zoom meeting. The conference will include presentations from our excellent speakers: Prof. Amin Setyo Leksono (Brawijaya University, INDONESIA) Assoc. Prof. Dr. Mohd. Nazre Saleh (Universiti Putra Malaysia, MALAYSIA) Dr. Linda May** (UK Centre for Ecology and Hidrology, UK) Prof. Dr. Ir. Y. Purwanto (MAB – UNESCO, LIPI, INDONESIA) Prof. Nguyen Hoang Tri** (Vietnam National University of Education, VIETNAM) Dr. Hans Thulstrup** (UNESCO, INDONESIA) Kunifumi Tagawa, Ph.D. (Hiroshima University, JAPAN) **in confirmation We promote “Integrating Conservation and Sustainable Development through Biosphere Reserves” as the conference theme. The conference will include presentations from plenary speakers, discussion in plenary sessions, and presentation of original research results related to topics: Agricultural Biodiversity, Conservation Ecology, Environmental Science, Sustainable Materials and Resources.
