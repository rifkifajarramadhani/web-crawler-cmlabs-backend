Jadwal Pelaksanaan TOEFL ITP Periode XIV September 2018 September 15th, 2018 TOEFL ITP TEST ANNOUNCEMENT IN UNIVERSITAS BRAWIJAYA PERIOD XIV September 2018 INBIS 2nd FLOOR The test of TOEFL ITP will be held on: Day    :  Tuesday Date    : September 18, 2018 Place    : INBIS 2nd FLOOR, VETERAN STREET, MALANG Time    : 7.30 am – end The test will be held in 2 sessions: Session I    : Tuesday, September 18, 2018, 7.30 am – 10.30 am, for 200 participants Session II   : Tuesday, September 18, 2018, 11.30 am – 2.30 pm, for 200 participants Every participant can only attend one session with the attached schedule [Schedule] Requirements Participants never taken UPB-UB TOEFL ITP test before Participants may only take UPB-UB TOEFL ITP test once Participants are required to be present 30 minutes before the test begin (7 am for the morning session, and 11 am for the noon session) Participants whose late to attend the test 15 minutes before the test started, and not filling the registration form, are considered as not attending.  His/her place can then be used by another participant on standby.
